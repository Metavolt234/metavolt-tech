Rice Selection Guide

Basmati Rice

Best for: Biryani, pilaf, and Persian dishes

Cooking tip: Soak for 30 minutes before cooking for best results

Jasmine Rice

Best for: Thai and Southeast Asian cuisine

Cooking tip: Use slightly less water than usual for perfect texture

Brown Rice

Best for: Healthy meals and weight management

Cooking tip: Requires longer cooking time and more water

Parboiled Rice

Best for: Everyday meals and fried rice

Cooking tip: Stays separate and fluffy, perfect for meal prep
