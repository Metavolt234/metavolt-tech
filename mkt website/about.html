Meet Our CEO

Kansiime Justus

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in the retail and wholesale industry, Kansiime Justus founded MKT Retail & Wholesalers with a simple vision: to provide quality products at affordable prices while supporting local communities.

Under his leadership, MKT has grown from a small neighborhood shop to one of Kyebando-Central's most trusted suppliers. His commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and community development has been the driving force behind our success.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Kansiime Justus, MKT Retail & Wholesalers began as a small family business in Kyebando-Central. With a vision to provide quality products at affordable prices, we've grown to become one of the most trusted suppliers in the region.
