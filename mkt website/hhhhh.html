Logo Design Philosophy

Each logo concept for Nzuri Care LLC incorporates elements that reflect care, wellness, and beauty ("nzuri" means beauty in Swahili). The designs use soft shapes, harmonious color palettes, and clean typography to convey professionalism and compassion.

The color schemes are chosen to evoke feelings of trust, calm, and vitality - essential qualities for a care-focused business.
