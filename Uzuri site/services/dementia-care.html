Dementia & Alzheimer's Care

Specialized care and support for individuals with memory loss and cognitive challenges

Specialized Memory Care

Our dementia care services are specifically designed to meet the unique needs of individuals with Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia. Our caregivers receive specialized training in dementia care techniques, communication strategies, and behavior management to provide compassionate, effective support.

Specialized Services Include:

  • Dementia-specific communication techniques
  • Memory stimulation activities
  • Behavior management and redirection
  • Safety supervision and wandering prevention
  • Routine establishment and maintenance
  • Medication management and reminders
  • Personal care assistance
  • Meal preparation and feeding assistance
  • Family education and support
  • 24/7 monitoring and support
