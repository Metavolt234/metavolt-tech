Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How do I get started with ComfortCare?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us to schedule a free in-home assessment. We'll meet with you and your loved one to discuss needs, answer questions, and create a personalized care plan.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Coeur d'Alene and surrounding communities in Kootenai County. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

Are your caregivers insured and bonded?

Yes, all ComfortCare caregivers are fully insured, bonded, and undergo thorough background checks for your peace of mind.

Can I change or adjust the care schedule?

Absolutely! We understand that needs can change. We offer flexible scheduling and can adjust care plans as needed with proper notice.
