Zam Kabahuma

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in healthcare management and senior care services, Zam founded Nzuri-Care with a vision to transform how we care for our aging population. Her passion for compassionate care stems from personal experience caring for her own grandparents.

Zam holds an Associate of Science in Medical Administration and maintains CPR, CMAA, and Child Development Psychology certifications. This educational foundation combines clinical knowledge, administrative expertise, and developmental insight, enabling a comprehensive approach to senior care that addresses both physical health and emotional well-being. Her multilingual skills further enhance culturally sensitive communication with diverse clients.
