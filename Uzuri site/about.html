Our Story

Compassionate care built on a foundation of trust and experience

Kabahuma Zam

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in healthcare management and senior care services, Zam founded Nzuri-Care with a vision to transform how we care for our aging population. Her passion for compassionate care stems from personal experience caring for her own grandparents.

Zam holds a Master's degree in Healthcare Administration and is a certified Geriatric Care Manager. Under her leadership, ComfortCare has grown to become one of the most trusted senior care providers in the region.

"At Nzuri-Care, we believe that every senior deserves to age with dignity, respect, and the highest quality of life possible. Our mission is to provide not just care, but genuine companionship and support for both our clients and their families."

- Kabahuma zam, CEO
